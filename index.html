Shallow Flood Events

Deeper floods from major events, like hurricanes, are less likely to occur, but cause greater damage than more shallow flood events, like heavy rains.

2030

Overall, Hoboken has a major risk of flooding over the next 30 years, which means flooding is likely to impact day to day life within the community.
